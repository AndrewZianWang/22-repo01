    Public Schools in Chicago

    Visualization 1: ISAT Exceeding % Comparison

    This bar chart compares the percentage of students exceeding ISAT standards in math and reading across all Chicago public schools. Each pair of bars represents a school’s performance in math and reading respectively. Schools are ordered along the x-axis.

    This chart focuses on high-performing schools—those with more than 50% of students exceeding standards in both math and reading. It highlights the top academic performers and allows quick visual comparison within this elite group.

    Visualization 4

    Map: School-Level Performance

    This interactive map (viz4_map_school_performance.html) shows the performance of individual Chicago public schools based on the average percentage of students exceeding standards in math and reading. Each dot represents a school. The size and color of the dots correspond to the performance level—larger and brighter dots indicate higher achieving schools.

    Map: Community-Level Performance

    This map (viz4_map_community_performance.html) aggregates performance by community area. It visualizes the average school performance within each neighborhood. Larger and more saturated markers represent communities with stronger academic achievement on average.
